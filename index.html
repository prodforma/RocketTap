About RocketTap

RocketTap is an engaging clicker game where players tap a rocket to earn clicks and coins. The game features.

RocketTap is a unique project created for all fans of clicker games. The main goal of the game is to tap on a rocket to earn clicks and coins. Players can enhance their abilities, complete daily tasks, earn rewards, and reach new heights in the game. Join us and start your space adventure with RocketTap!
